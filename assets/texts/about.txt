About CollectiFair

Goals
CollectiFair connects people who love collectible and themed goods. We make it simple to discover unique items, share listings, and grow a trusted community around hobbies and passions.

Audience
- Collectors looking for rare or hard-to-find items
- Sellers who want to reach buyers interested in niche categories
- Enthusiasts who enjoy LEGO, board games, comics, figurines, souvenirs, and fan merchandise

Platform highlights
- Focused categories — no unrelated mass-market listings
- Clear navigation for buyers and sellers
- Easy listing and search tools
- Direct communication between users
- Optional paid promotion for faster sales
- A community built around shared interests
