Question: Do I have to pay to publish a listing?
Answer: No. Basic listing placement is free. Paid services are optional tools that help your listing get more visibility when you want to sell faster.

Question: What paid services are available?
Answer: You can choose color highlighting, daily bumps in the list, pinning to the top of a category, home page placement, or the “Premium” package that includes all features.

Question: How long can I promote a listing?
Answer: Each service can be purchased for 1, 2, 10, or 30 days. Select the duration that fits your selling goals and budget.

Question: How is the cost calculated?
Answer: The price depends on the service you choose and the number of days. Use the price list to compare options and see the total cost before activating a service.

Question: Can I combine several paid services?
Answer: Yes. You can activate individual services or use the “Premium” package, which includes all promotion options at a better combined price.

Question: When does paid promotion start?
Answer: Promotion begins as soon as the service is activated for your listing and remains active for the selected number of days.

Question: What if my item sells before the promotion period ends?
Answer: That’s great news. You can close or remove the listing at any time. Any unused promotion time simply stops when the listing is no longer active.

Question: Are paid services worth it?
Answer: They are especially useful for rare, high-value, or competitive items. Sellers who use promotion tools often receive more views and faster responses.
